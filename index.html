letsVibe

is a chrome extension that allows a peer to peer video syncing platform for watching movies or tv-series from media streaming platforms like Netflix or Hulu with an alternate enhanced 3D experience and also supports live streams, sports and many more.

“A realistic 3D augmented virtual theater”
